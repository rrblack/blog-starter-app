<h1 className="text-red-600"> Welcome back after nearly 2 years </h1>

This has definietly been the longest I have gone without a blog post. But it is for a good reason. I have been super busy ever since my last update almost 2 years ago. To start we are now in IT. Yes that's right, I am no longer a teacher, no longer an editor. I am in IT. Working at the lowest tier of IT currently, mind you, but we are out here. I have a week of night shift in a row every month. And it just so happens that week is starting up again in 2 days. So while I currently am forcing myself to stay up till 6am today to adjust for my shifts starting Sunday, I figured I would get my first blog post out on my new site up.

Speaking of this site, what do you guys think? We are no longer on Wordpress. It's even better; I build this site completely by myself. Yes that's right, this site was built entirely by me from the ground up. Sure I used a starting template, but everything you see here is specifically because I wanted it to be there. I designed every part that you see on this site. From the comment section to the mailing list, title sizes, you name it. Working in IT has been a propelling force that has pushed me to constantly want to learn and create new things. With my current role in IT being fairly easy, there is a lot of downtime. And in that downtime I am busy working away at studying for certifications, learning how to program a site or set up my own completelty self hosted photo library. I have a ton of projects in the works and everday is a new adventure. This blog was just one of many I plan on doing. 

Also, since this site is completely owned AND hosted by me now. That means no more yearly $100 fees from Wordpress, no more slow site loading, no more targeting advertising. If there is any features you guys want on the site leave me a comment and I will literally create it and add it on the site. There are no longer any limits. For any nerds who are curious what language I am running this site on, it is written in Next.js, Typescript and Markup. Also for those who were wondering where my old blogs all went. **They are still all here**, don't worry! You can access them on the front page on this site. While I currently don't have plans to migrate them to this site, it is not out of the realm of possibility in the eventual future. Keep in mind this blog is still a work in progress and I will be continuously maintaining it and adding new features when I can. 

I have a huge back log of stories the share these past 2 years but I can update you all when I have time during the night shift \:) 

And yes, for anyone still wondering I am still in the same relationship I was back in my first blog post. Yukina and I have been together for nearly 2 years now. December 17th will be our 2 year anniversary. I dont think I would have made it this far without her help and support. She was the one afterall who suggested that I enter IT in the first place. She also helped me tremenseously when I was unemployed trying to get a job in IT.
